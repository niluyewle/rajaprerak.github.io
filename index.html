About

I am an Assistant Professor (tenure-Track) at the Department of Biosystems Engineering and Soil Science, University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

My research focuses on enhancing grain quality, agricultural productivity, profitability, and environmental sustainability through the use of innovative design, modeling, and engineering methodologies. With a multidisciplinary background in agriculture, I have developed a versatile skill set. Recently, I have been focusing on using advanced imaging techniques to detect and manage plant early diseases on soyabean and corn, ensuring healthier crops and better yields.
